About Us:

At Lean Cuts, we exist to create good barbeque and big smiles. Our passion comes from humble beginnings at a small farm in Iowa. From a state where pigs outnumber people we know the value of good pork. We know that nothing beats a nicely cut and evenly cooked peice of meat after a long day which is exactly the experience we want to provide to our customers.
